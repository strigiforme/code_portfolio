A Brief Introduction

This document is the style guide for my website, howardpearce.ca. It is intended to guide the design and development of my website, but also serves to guide myself and decisions I make in order to become closer to the ideals I hold closest.

Keeping a strong and congruous identity is an essential step in building a brand. While this identity may change with time, it's important to act in line with your current beliefs and intentions in order to construct a strong force of personality. If done properly, you/your brand will be viewed as dependable, competent, and authentic. In turn, you will find yourself the same.
